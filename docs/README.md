# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

Welcome! This README provides an overview of project management at OctoAcme and quick access to all detailed process documents stored in this folder. Whether you are a new team member getting oriented or a seasoned contributor looking for a specific workflow, start here.

## Summary of Project Management Processes

OctoAcme employs a structured yet flexible project management approach designed to ensure efficient delivery, stakeholder alignment, and continuous improvement across cross-functional teams. The process initiates with clearly defined steps for project validation and stakeholder alignment, using a "Project One-pager" to capture the problem statement, success metrics, stakeholder roles, and high-level timelines. Once a project is approved, planning focuses on breaking work into actionable, prioritized increments, identifying risks and dependencies, and establishing a release plan with well-defined milestones and backlog items. Risk management is woven through the lifecycle, with processes for identification, assessment, mitigation, and regular monitoring tracked via a risk register and routine updates.

Critical to OctoAcme's success is the definition and utilization of core personas: Project Managers, Product Managers, Developers, QA/Testers, and Stakeholders. Each role carries specific responsibilities—for instance, the Project Manager orchestrates plans, timelines, risk mitigation, and inter-team communication, while Product Managers focus on defining value, prioritizing roadmaps, and iterating based on evidence. Developers take charge of implementation and test coverage, collaborating closely with QA to uphold quality standards. These roles interact through structured ceremonies such as kickoffs, standups, planning sessions, and retrospectives, ensuring clarity, ownership, and accountability throughout the delivery cycle.

Communication underpins OctoAcme projects, with standardized cadences like weekly syncs between Product and Project Managers, twice-weekly standups for the delivery team, and monthly stakeholder updates. The documentation emphasizes transparent reporting via dashboards, project boards, and consistent status templates, providing single sources of truth for all participants. Risks and incident communications are escalated through well-defined paths, balancing swift resolution with blameless post-incident reviews to foster a culture of psychological safety and learning.

Quality assurance at OctoAcme is built into each phase of the process. From the onset, acceptance criteria and "Definition of Done" are established to guide implementation and testing. Automated CI pipelines enforce quality gates for unit, integration, and security tests, with manual QA reserved for feature acceptance when needed. Releases follow a disciplined checklist, including staged deployments, smoke tests, and rollback plans. After each milestone or incident, retrospectives capture and prioritize actionable improvements, solidifying a commitment to constant iteration and elevated delivery standards.
